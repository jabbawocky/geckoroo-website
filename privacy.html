Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Overview

At GeckoRoo, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Data We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Email address: Only if you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Contact information: If you email us directly

Information collected automatically

  • Basic analytics: Page views, referring sites, browser type
  • Device information: Screen size, operating system (for site optimization)
  • IP address: For geographic analytics (country/region level only)

We use privacy-focused analytics that don't track you across the web. No personal information is stored in our analytics.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies to improve your experience:

  • Theme preference: Remembers if you prefer light or dark mode
  • Functional cookies: Required for site features to work properly

We don't use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We don't sell your data to third parties.

Affiliate Links Disclosure

GeckoRoo participates in affiliate programs. When you click on product links and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

When you click an affiliate link:

  • You'll be redirected to the merchant's website
  • The merchant may set their own cookies
  • Your interactions on their site are governed by their privacy policy
  • We don't have access to your purchase information or payment details

We currently work with affiliate programs including but not limited to Temu, Amazon, and other retail partners. Each merchant has their own privacy policy which you should review.

How We Use Your Data

We use collected information to:

  • Send newsletter updates (only if you've subscribed)
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Improve our website and content
  • Understand which products and categories are most popular
  • Ensure the site works properly on different devices

Data Sharing

We don't sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • With service providers who help run our site (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to:

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Keep your devices and browsers updated
  • Be cautious about sharing personal information online

Your Rights

Under Australian privacy law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of incorrect information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC)

Children's Privacy

GeckoRoo is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we've collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be noted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: hello@geckoroo.com.au

We aim to respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days.

Privacy Complaints
