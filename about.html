About GeckoRoo

cheap gear, rough builds, good fun.

Our Story

Started in a shed in regional Australia, GeckoRoo began when we got tired of reading "reviews" that were just rewritten product descriptions. We wanted to know: does this gear actually work when you're covered in sawdust, or camping in the rain, or trying to fix something at 11pm on a Sunday?

So we started buying budget gear and putting it through its paces. Some of it surprised us. A lot of it didn't. But we found enough gems to make it worth sharing.

We're not professional reviewers or influencers. We're just people who use tools, go camping, and occasionally need a gadget that won't break the bank. If something survives our everyday abuse, we figure it's worth telling you about.

How We Earn

Let's be upfront: This site contains affiliate links. When you buy something through our links, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps keep the site running and lets us buy more gear to test.

But here's our promise: we only recommend stuff we'd actually use ourselves. If something's rubbish, we won't list it just to make a few dollars. There's enough junk on the internet already.

Every product we feature has been:

  • Purchased with our own money or tested extensively
  • Used in real-world conditions for at least a few weeks
  • Compared against similar products in its price range
  • Evaluated for actual value, not just low price
